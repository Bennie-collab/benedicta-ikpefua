I'm a creative with hands-on experience in graphic design, video editing, and social media. Having worked as a Virtual Assistant for a social media manager, I got to do a bit of everything: writing captions, coming up with content ideas, and supporting the visual side of things.

I bring a clean, intentional eye to everything I create and I enjoy working in fast-paced digital spaces where good visuals actually make a difference. Canva and CapCut are my go-to tools and I believe great content doesn't have to be complicated to be effective.
